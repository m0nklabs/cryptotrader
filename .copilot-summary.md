### CoinDossier Alignment (Issue #222)

The issue comment mentions CoinDossier as a downstream consumer. Current mapping:

**Role verdicts produce:**
- action: "BUY" | "SELL" | "NEUTRAL" | "VETO"

- reasoning: str
- metrics: dict[str, float]

- action: "BUY" | "SELL" | "HOLD" | "AVOID"

- risk_level: "low" | "medium" | "high" | "extreme"

- take_profit: [tp1, tp2]
